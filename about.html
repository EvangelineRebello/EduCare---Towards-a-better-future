Educare is India’s leading independent educational NGO, which works in 18 states of the country. Beginning its journey in 2001 in India, and registered as ‘EduCare’ also known as 'Bal Shiksha' in some areas, we have changed the lives of more than 11 million (1.1 crore) children.


We firmly believe that every child deserves the best chance for a bright future, which is why, irrespective of religion, caste, creed, race, ethnicity or gender, EduCare's Fund foundation extends a helping hand to anyone and everyone who deserve it.


In India and across the world, EduCare works on-ground – every day and especially, in times of crisis. Our pioneering programs address children’s unique needs; giving them a healthy start, an opportunity to learn as well as protection from harm.


Every child born has the right to be educated. The main objective of our organization is to educate each child and in turn develop the society and the world.
